The flight to Calgary {start}

D016-02 02-07-27 - Westering via Westjet from Hamilton. We do all the booking for this trip ourselves, mostly on the Internet and by long-distance telephone: airline tickets, hotels and rental cars. D016-20 02-07-28 p3 - Banff townsite, from Sulphur Mountain Our first whole day in Banff is devoted to exploring the town and surroundings. D016-18 02-07-28 - Banff Gondola up Sulphur Mountain Sanson Peak is 7,449ft., but the trail is easy going under foot. We fudge a little and take this gondola on the descent. If you walk up, you get a free ride down.

D016-34 02-07-28 - Sanson Peak observatory Quoting from an internet source: "The peak is named after Norman Sanson who was the curator and meteorologist for the Banff Park Museum. One of his duties was to climb up the mountain to the observatory to record weather data. He logged over 1000 trips the last of which was in 1945."

D016-38 02-07-28 - Banff hot springs Your reward for ascending Sanson Peak is the gondola ride down, followed by a dip in the hot springs pool. At certain seasons, there's very little natural hot water and they have to supply artificial heat.

D016-39 02-07-28 - The Rimrock Hotel The Rimrock, looking like a pretty good CPR hotel imitation, is situated just below the hot springs pool. Cascade Mountain is in the background.
