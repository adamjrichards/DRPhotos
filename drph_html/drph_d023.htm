These were taken with an Olympus D-40 digital camera on a miniature tripod. Activated by the camera's remote control device.
Complications: The camera must be "on" all the time for the remote control to work. This rapidly uses up the battery. I took about 30 minutes to get these pictures. Then the camera shut itself down. An outboard 3 volt power supply would help.
The effective range of the remote control is about 5 feet. And it has to be aimed from the front of the camera, so the lens has to be pointing towards you. This means you have to sit close to the subject and wait very, very quietly.
