Dilemmas, choices, decisions
Agonizing decisions. The central picture could be a path, a bridge, leading to a destination. Anything that suggests a decision: fork, "V", acute angle.
The above suggests a new category of interesting subject matter: not only paths that lead ever onward, but bridges, forks in the road, even bifurcated trees.
Could this sort of thinking about pictures be called "conceptual"?
