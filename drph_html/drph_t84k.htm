Eastern Newfoundland
The older communities of the east are in sharp contrast to the west coast. Anyone looking for picturesque decrepitude, will find it in some some of the old piers and fishing sheds of Twillingate. But surprisingly, most of the houses are freshly shingled, painted and furnished in Sear's Catalogue style.
