D252-203A V13-06-10 - Storefront, photo sketch FX The "photo sketch" treatment uses DR's PhotoShop macros to create an edge outline, plus a softening of image areas within the outline to reduce merely textural detail. The goal is to created a more iconic version of the image, coresponding roughly to the technique of an artist who creates a pen or pencil outline, then applies colour and shading using a broader application of pencil or brush. Pictures using this treatment frequently resemble pen or ink outline sketches to which a water-colour wash has been applied.
