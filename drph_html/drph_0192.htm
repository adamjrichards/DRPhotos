0192-38 80-08 - Last view from Boote Spur
Below this point the view diminishes. Fatigue sets in and the gaze moves from distant horizons to rocks and tree roots in the downward path. We arrive back at the parking lot about 7pm, with the sun well down behind the peaks. Altogether, 12 hours of walking and an estimated 9000 feet of up and down traverse. We next find an upscale steak house where we demolish chunks of raw beef and a bottle of Chateau Pomeroy red. Finally, our tent and sleeping bags welcome us.
