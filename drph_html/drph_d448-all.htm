D448-053 17-08-03 - Complete with buffalo skin robe. I recall being driven out into the country from Danville in winter, to a farmland bingo game in a wood-heated, kerosine-lit house. The horse had to bash its way through chest-high snow drifts on unploughed country roads. We kids ran wild. My fave game was crawling under the bingo table down an aisle of adult legs.
