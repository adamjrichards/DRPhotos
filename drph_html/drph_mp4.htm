MP4 movie versions of WWWB automated slide shows.


WAV sound transcriptions of 33rpm LPs.

The following video pages seem to handle better on my Samsung phone using the Firefox browser, plus the "video player" option. This player begins to play the movie immediately instead of first downloading the whole file, then providing the play option after a waiting period. But on my desktop computer, the Chrome browser provides the "play immediately" option. It appears that browser and HTML developers haven't yet achieved consistency in this regard.


These video shows originated as WWWB automated 35mm slide shows with synchronized sound. They were digitized using screen recorder software.
