D061-17 03-07-08 - Welland River layover.
Not an exceptional picture, really, but something haunting about the combination of knees, book, and river obscured by leaves. The handwriting, the drawing and the knees suggest to me a mood of experiencing, of perceiving that is quite vivid. This more dimly suggests ways of including art and narrative in a photo, of blending the two modes of seeing. But how to do this in a way that is interesting to the general viewer. Perhaps bring a much bigger sketchbook, even collage materials, plein air scribbling, scrapbooking while the camera looks on. And participates, even. Can we imagine a portable printer spitting out the same image while the camera looks on?
