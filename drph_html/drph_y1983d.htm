y1983 -
The partial account of a research trip by car, west and along the 49th parallel, to gather impressions for my novel The Scarlet Thread. The main character is young James Fielding (JF) who is part of a British expedition to gather intelligence on the vaguely defined frontier area between British and US territory. Part of their mission is to shadow the route of a similar American expedition whose real purpose is to provide support for Jeffersonian expansionism.
