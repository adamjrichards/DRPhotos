D399-022 16-07-20 - Toured the P and W engine plant.
* Pratt and Whitney engine reconditioning plant in Longeuil Quebec. It was on a high school career day. Facinated by an indoor test bunker with a glass window where they could rev up engines and check for issues. They ran an engine for us to see and hear. I recall also them showing how a propeller was tested for balance to prevent vibration. A single penny, stuck on one blade could put it dangerously out of balance.
