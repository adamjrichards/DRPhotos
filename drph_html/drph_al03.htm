Enid, Scott, Lucy H., Derek, Albert H., Eric. 1947? Westmore and Somerled.
I remember this visit quite well. It was early in the year. I sat in one of those folding chairs in the evening and listened to a robin sing, thinking that never was anything so sweet. There were balconies on the back of the apartment building where the cliffs lived. One of their neighbours upstairs had trained the cat to step into a peach basket lowered to the ground on a cord. The cat was then brought up to the balcony without anyone having to go downstairs to let it in. On another visit there were massive grass fires in the field behind that hedge on the left. The flames shot up above tree level.
With Fraser, muriel and Wendy Cliff, Eric taking the picture.
