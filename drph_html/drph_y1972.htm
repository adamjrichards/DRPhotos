1972-10 - First hike St. Catharines.

0035-01 72-10 -
There was actually an old family cemetery, or at least, a monument, up behind Briardale school. Subsequently moved and the fence removed.
In our early days in St. Catharines we were mightily impressed by the surrounding suburban green spaces. In Toronto we had to make lengthy car or transit trips to reach similar areas. These wanderings still strike me as the beginning of our (my?) reaquaintance with the outdoors. This was something that seemed restricted, out of reach in Toronto, even though we loved going to the Toronto Island and High Park.
