This version specially set up for printing. rotate "q" 180 deg, 1 pixel black border, 264 white space on left, 40 white on the bottom. This allows max size printing in Panavue image assembler.The bottom edge of the image comes out the right hand side of the printer. The paper must be tight to this side with a big enough margin for mounting. The top edge (bottom of the image) with no white space allowance will be tight against the A3 paper margin. A3 is 297mm wide. But since we are using 13" roll paper, 330mm, this will leave about 1 inch extra space on the left, which is actually the bottom of the original image. This can't be helped with existing software, but is probably useful for attaching or writing-in captions.
