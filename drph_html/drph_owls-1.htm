Owls, with recorded commentary (original)

Scans are from early black and white hand-scanner. Same time as Temple Tikvah bulletin.

OWL1 - Barred Owl

The Barred owl is forever debarred from polite society. No one knows why. And the owl is wisely saying nothing.

OWL10 - Temperance Owl

This Temperance owl is guarding an empty glass -- misguidedly.

OWL11 - Bon-vivant Owl

The Bon-vivant owl has had a few too many.

OWL3 - Long-eared Owl

The Long-eared owl has a knack for overhearing gossip.

OWL4 - Great Grey Owl

The Great Grey owl is neither great nor gray ...

OWL2 - Great Grey Owl, surprised

but surprised that you think so.

OWL5 - Owl of Athena

The Owl of Athena is worried that you may find out the secret of its wisdom ...

OWL5R - Owl of Athena, reversed

which is, that it has eyes, like the god Janus, on both sides of its head.

OWL7 - Predatory Owl

The Predatory owl thinks that a mouse dinner would be a real hoot.

OWL8 - Inebriated Owl

The Inebriated owl is a close relative of the Bon-vivant owl, but has given up all hope of respectability.

OWL9 - Bump-on-a-log Owl

The Bump-on-a-log owl gets a reputation for wisdom by merely looking

confused and saying very little -- wisely.
