2017-05-20 Waiting for the ferry. Before starting on our zodiac trip we have to wait for the ferry to deliver another tripper from the other side of the water. Unfortunately, someone on the ferry staff hadn't shown up for their shift, and Transport Canada regulations forbid the ferry crossing short-staffed. So we wait on a picturesque beach while our tour operator sorts things out.
