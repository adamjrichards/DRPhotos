D205-014 08-03-01 - Pretend we're enjoying ourselves.
Smile for the press. But even 10 measely kilos eventually proves too much and Derek develops a limp. This results from his 1990s sciatic injury. Luckily, he was able to reduce the load by dumping the contents of a large water bottle in his backpack. Put there, not for hydration, but to test carrying ability, plus the advantage of a "plan-B", rapid weight reduction option. This avoided the need to scrap expensive hiking equipment. Sometimes DR's native pessimism turns out for the best, people.
