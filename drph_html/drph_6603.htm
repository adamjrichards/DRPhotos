Excursion with Bill Brooks out on the Tantramar Marsh. The canal was begun and construction advanced when the principals went bankrupt. It would have saved small coasting vessels the time, expense and hazard of a trip round the whole of Nova Scotia. Assuming that you wanted to get from the Bay of Fundy to the Strait of Northumberland, looking across at P.E.I.
