* One of the airmen at St Hubert had a Tiger Moth that he kept on the military airfield. The RCAF encouraged this in moderation as a way of building up aeronautical skills.
* Sat in one of those on an Airforce Day long ago.
* Pratt and Whitney engine reconditioning plant in Longeuil Quebec. It was on a high school career day. Facinated by an indoor test bunker with a glass window where they could rev up engines and check for issues. They ran an engine for us to see and hear. I recall also them showing how a propeller was tested for balance to prevent vibration. A single penny, stuck on one blade could put it dangerously out of balance. Back to top
