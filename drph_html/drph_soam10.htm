D227-321 09-06-24 -

D227-322 09-06-24 - J.C. is not amused.
We wonder how much this festival represents present day belief. The crowd is nominaly Catholic. But the fervour of the event is more than merely folkloric pageant. The statue of J.C. was donated by Palestinian Christians.
