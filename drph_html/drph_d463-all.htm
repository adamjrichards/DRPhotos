D463-106 17-10-02 - Lunch overlooking the site. I found this a very impressive place, despite the fact that the stonework is so amorphous. A kind of numinous feeling, as at Stonehenge, or on the summit of Kilimanjaro, and at the Sun gate, just befor we entered Macchu Picchu. A feeling of being briefly but tenuously in touch with ... the ground of existence. Presumptuous thought? Must check out my novel, "Premonitions of the past," where I try to deal with this mysterious feeling, but in a somewhat offhand way.
