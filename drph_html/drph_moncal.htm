Monitor calibration
07-02-07: Another attempt at monitor calibration on NED: Hdw flat, ie. Samsung synchmaster 225bw set at brightness 100, contrast 100. ATI interface: gamma 1.1, brightness 10, contrast 50. This agrees with calibration charts. See bookmarked websites. 07-02-08: redid with g=1.0. Note that "contrast" on the hardware and ATI interfaces is really brightness or whitepoint, "brightness" is really black level or blackpoint. This seems to clarify things, the main thing to do is to set black and white-points of the monitor to correspond with the image's black 0,0,0 and white 255,255,255 levels. Gradations should be visible at both ends. Updated this note: 08-03-03.
