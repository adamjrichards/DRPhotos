D227-007 09-06-22 - On the train, Machu Picchu to Cuzco.

We arrive in Cuzco in the dark, are bused to the hotel by Metropolitan Tours. We sleep the sleep of Inca Trail hikers dreaming of ancient ruins. ... In two days, the Inti Raymi festival begins, another show.
