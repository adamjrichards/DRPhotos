July 11, Wednesday.
* Back to Gargantua Bay.
* Hectic wave conditions.
* No time for photos.
* Pack up and back on the road.
* Camp at Pukaskwa National Park.
* To Marathon to recuperate.
We head back to Gargantua Bay. Next day weather conditions looked steady enough for a retreat back south to base camp in the Gargantua parking lot. The breeze was strong, but mainly behind us. Waves were high on a few stretches of open water. At one point we were nearly surfing. In the trough you couldn't see shore. On the crest you had a fine view. Paddling conditions were too hectic and spashy to leave off paddling even for a few seconds, so I got no pictures this leg of the trip. Packing up we drove an hour on the beach access road back to the Trans-Canada highway and north to Pukasaw National Park. Camping at Hattie Cove we drive into Marathon to recuperate.
