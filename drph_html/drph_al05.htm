AL05-026A 40-to-49 - R100. St. Hubert mooring tower. Pic taken by friend of Eric and Enid. July-August 1930. The base of this building was still extant when we lived in St. Hubert, late 40's. The tower was probably removed as a hazard to the RCAF planes that used the nearby runways. I also recall giant iron stanchions and chain links in a field in another part of the airfield, probably used as tail-end tie-downs for the dirigeable.
