6602-055 54-to-58 - Young Winegrowers tour group.
Through the 3 F Wing airbase education office at Zweibrucken, I was invited to go on a youth trip with the Young Winegrowers of St. Martin a.d. Weinstrasse. Our route took us through southern France, northern Italy, back across the Alps to Switzerland, then home again to Germany. This picture was taken after the trip.
