Scanned on lo-res scanner, purchased at Staples. Claims 5 mpixels. Rescan on Minolta if higher res desired. Dust, mould, colour balance, focus and framing issues. Slides in trays are sometimes in strange order. Scanned one tray = one portfolio. After scanning, slides were stored in bulk, grouped by the original trays in the order scanned. This should permit retrieval for rescanning, with only brief searching.

Each picture, marked "#", meaning "selected", has been adjusted using a PhotoShop macro, F8, which improves colour, contrast and sharpness. Pictures have to be rescanned to revert to the original raw scan, no .JPGs were saved, as with normal camera files.
