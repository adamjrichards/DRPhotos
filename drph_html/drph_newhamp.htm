D101-023 04-08-17 - Mt. Washinton, from Mt. Monroe.
Mount Washington summit on the top right. A plume of smoke from the cog railway on centre horizon. This was our best day. The weather was perfect. We had lunch in Lake of the Clouds hut. Energy levels brimming again. It was one of those glorious interludes that mountain hikers often experience. A feeling, almost, of physical intimacy with the vast bulk of the mountain, of belonging, of being home, of being immortal, for a few brief hours.
