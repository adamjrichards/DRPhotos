We enter the fairgrounds. The Brigden Agricultural Exhibition has been going since 1850, 158 years of operation. This was 17 years before Confederation, when memories of the Mackenzie rebellion were still fresh.

D216-054 08-10-12 - First prize.

D216-057 08-10-12 - Champion corn.
Derek recalls days at the autumn school fair when he lived in Danville, Quebec. Students were bussed to the town of Asbestos from all over the county. When their produce, corn, carrots, radishes, tomatoes, was handed in to the judges, they were kept busy with cartoons in a local theatre and lunch. Afterwards,the results of the judging were shown back in the arena. Derek remembers a few honourable mentions.
